My Future in IT?

    Why I choose IT?

    Why I choose IT? There are two reason why I choose IT out of all the courses in college. The first one is, when I graduated from high school, I have no idea what strand I will take in senior high school and still pursue it in college. I do not have any interest in any course in senior high school. So, I did some research on the internet about 'In demand and high paying jobs in the Philippines'. After searching on different sites, most jobs are inclined to technology, which I agreed on because our technology is advancing so fast day by day. Second, after I graduated from senior high school, I'm having second thoughts if I will still pursue IT in college. Just like I said, I feel IT is not for me. But then, curiosity got me when I was watching gameplays. I find it fascinating that game developers can be creative on developing a game. So I said to my inner self, 'I have to learn how to develop a game on my own'. And that is the two mainly reason why I choose IT.

    My Future in IT Industry

    If you love the things that you do, you will never get tired of it. My future in IT industry is gonna be tiring to my future self, I know it. Facing my desktop, doing programming for work because it is due tomorrow, learning programming languages and developing my own game so I can do what my goal is at the first place on why I choose IT, and repeat every other day. I can still see myself in IT industry in the near future, and still discovering new fields in IT. Overall, my future in IT industry is gonna be hard and tiring, mainly after I graduated in college and finding a job. It is gonna be competitive in the IT industry and it belongs on facing the responsibilities as an adult.
